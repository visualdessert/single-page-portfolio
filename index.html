LPU Pirates Esports and BS Esport Project

The Esports Summit, consisting of Lyceum of the Philippines University - Manila's Game Development and Design (GDD), and Esports Management (EM) students, is dedicated to developing talent and learning within the Bachelor of Science in Esports Program. As the students have a strong passion for advancing in the Esports industry, they aim to make the Esports Summit, Re: Volt, a crucial platform for sharing Esports education.
